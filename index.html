How to Smoke Basic

Here we want to go over the basic of how to smoke basic since we have covered the basics of what they are and most used and favor of the kind I have found in Louisville, Ky.

With this in play, we will start to build up tolerance at the same time, so be aware that you may need to start smoking more, and or think you are using more, but don't worry we are teaching you to save.
